Paneer Butter Masala is a popular and indulgent North Indian dish known for its rich and creamy tomato-based gravy. This vegetarian delight features cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) simmered in a luscious blend of tomatoes, cream, and aromatic spices. The dish is characterized by its vibrant orange-red color, owing to the use of tomatoes and Kashmiri red chili powder.
